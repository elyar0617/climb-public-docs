# Climb Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 11, 2026

Climb is a debt payoff planning app that helps users organize debts, choose a payoff strategy, and track progress toward reducing debt. This policy explains what information Climb uses and how that information is handled.

## Information You Enter

Climb may ask you to enter financial planning information, including:

- Monthly income
- Monthly expenses
- Credit score, if you choose to provide it
- Debt Attack Budget
- Debt names
- Debt balances
- APRs
- Minimum payments
- Credit limits
- Debt types
- Autopay status, amount, and frequency
- Overdue status

You may also import debt information from a CSV file that you select.

## How Climb Uses This Information

Climb uses the information you provide to:

- Create a debt payoff plan
- Rank debts by Snowball, Avalanche, or Climb strategy
- Show required payments, extra attack payments, and total planned payments
- Estimate progress and milestones
- Provide plan improvement guidance

Climb provides planning guidance only. It does not provide professional financial, legal, tax, or credit advice.

## Local Storage

Climb stores your plan data locally on your device using app storage. This lets the app restore your plan after you close and reopen it.

At this time, Climb does not use:

- A login or account system
- Cloud sync
- Bank connections
- Payment processing
- Advertising networks

## CSV Import

Climb may allow you to import debts from a CSV file that you choose from your device. Imported CSV data is used to create debt entries in your local Climb plan.

Climb does not upload the selected CSV file to a server.

## Sharing And Selling Data

Climb does not currently sell personal data.

Climb does not currently share your financial plan data with third parties.

If future versions add optional cloud sync, analytics, account features, or other third-party services, this policy should be updated before those features are released.

## Deleting Your Data

You can delete saved Climb data from the app by using Reset Saved Data in Settings. This removes the locally saved plan and progress data from the device.

You can also remove app data by uninstalling Climb or clearing app storage through Android system settings.

## Children's Privacy

Climb is not intended for children under 13. The app is designed for people managing personal financial obligations. Climb does not knowingly collect information from children.

## Security

Climb keeps plan data in local app storage on your device. You are responsible for keeping your device secure, including using a screen lock and keeping your operating system up to date.

No method of storage is completely secure, but Climb is designed to avoid unnecessary data collection and does not currently transmit your plan data to a cloud service.

## Changes To This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as Climb changes. If new data collection, cloud sync, analytics, ads, accounts, or third-party services are added, this policy should be updated to reflect those changes.
